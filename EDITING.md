# Editing the site — the 5-minute guide for Teri

Once the site is set up, every change happens in one place: **teritowner.ca/admin**.

## How to log in

1. Go to **teritowner.ca/admin**
2. Enter your email + password
3. You're in

Forgot your password? Click **Forgot password** on the login screen — you'll get an email.

## The dashboard

On the left you'll see categories:

- **Site Settings** — colors, your email, social links, financial agent info, form URLs
- **News Posts** — the blog
- **Events** — upcoming events shown on the News page
- **Endorsements** — quotes from supporters
- **Press & Media** — links to articles about you
- **Platform Pillars** — the 3 priority areas shown on the homepage

## How to write a news post (2 minutes)

1. Click **News Posts** → **New Post** (green button, top right).
2. Fill in:
   - **Title** — the headline
   - **Date** — today's date
   - **Excerpt** — 1–2 sentence preview
   - **Header image** — optional; click to upload from your computer
   - **Body** — write your post. Toolbar at the top lets you add bold, links, headings, lists, images.
3. Make sure **Draft** is OFF (toggle should be grey, not green).
4. Click **Publish** at the top.
5. Done! Your post appears on the site in about 1 minute.

## How to add an event

Same as a post, but under **Events**. Fill in the start date/time, end time (optional), and location.

Events automatically appear on the News & Events page, ordered by date. Past events hide themselves.

## How to add an endorsement

1. **Endorsements → New Endorsement**
2. Name, role (e.g. "Mom of three, Eagle Mountain"), the quote itself
3. **Order** — lower number shows up first (1 = top)
4. Optional photo
5. Publish

## How to change colors or navigation

1. **Site Settings → site**
2. Scroll down to **Colors** — change any of the hex codes
3. Scroll to **Navigation** — reorder, rename, or add menu items
4. Publish
5. The site updates in ~1 minute

## How to change the donation link

1. **Site Settings → site**
2. Find **Donation link**
3. Paste the URL
4. Publish

The "Donate" buttons on the site will now link there.

## Safety net

Every edit is saved in a place called GitHub automatically. If you ever break something:

- Tell Chris — he can undo any change in about 30 seconds.
- Or in Netlify → **Deploys** → find the last good version → **Publish deploy**. That instantly rolls back.

**You can't permanently break anything.** So experiment freely.

## What editors CANNOT do via /admin (and who to call)

- Change the overall layout / page structure → ask Chris
- Change the homepage hero photo → ask Chris (hard-coded, takes 2 min)
- Add a whole new page (e.g. "Endorsements from Businesses") → ask Chris

Everything else — text, colors, links, news, events, endorsements, press — is yours.
